# Trade-Specific Keywords & Phrases

This document contains focused keywords and short phrases for each trade in the SMS Andon system. Each entry uses realistic language that USA home builders and field subcontractors would actually use in texts, emails, or voice messages.

## How to Use

- Each trade has two tables: **Keywords** (single words or very short terms) and **Phrases** (short descriptions, 2–15 words).
- The **Severity** column is left blank so you can mark each entry as **[ ] Yellow** or **[ ] Red** based on your operational experience.
- **Red:** Serious issues that usually block progress, create safety risks, cause major delays, or prevent the next trade from starting.
- **Yellow:** Problems that are concerning but not immediately blocking (e.g. quality issues, cleanliness, minor delays).
